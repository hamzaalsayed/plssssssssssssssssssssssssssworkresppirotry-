ABOUT US

The beginning of Qasr ElKbabgi dates back to the year 2018 in the city of Tanta, as a response to our observation of the Egyptian restaurant scene's need for a new approach that combines the elegance of the venue with the provision of genuine quality and service for Eastern Food in general, and Egyptian Food in particular. The vision of Mr. Ahmed El-Baz (businessman and chairman of the board of Qasr ElKbabgi restaurants) to make a breakthrough in the Eastern Food sector in Egypt stemmed from his consideration as a lover and connoisseur of Egyptian food. His ability to make a difference was rooted in the understanding that the discerning public's needs are best known by someone with a refined taste and a genuine love for Eastern cuisine.

Our signature dishes

Kabab El Farha:

Kabab El Farah is a celebratory Middle Eastern dish featuring tender skewers of marinated lamb or beef, grilled to perfection and served with fragrant rice, grilled vegetables, and a tangy tahini sauce. The meat is meticulously seasoned with a blend of aromatic spices, herbs, and citrus, creating a complex and mouthwatering flavor profile, while the expert grilling technique produces a delectable char on the outside while keeping the interior juicy and tender. Fluffy, spice-infused rice and an array of grilled seasonal vegetables accompany the rich and savory kababs, with the crowning touch being the tangy and creamy tahini sauce, which provides a lively contrast to the robust flavors and makes this dish perfect for bringing people together around the table for special occasions.

Royal Mixed Grill Feast:

The "Royal Mixed Grill Feast" is a grand showcase of Middle Eastern flavors, featuring expertly grilled meats. It includes lamb chops, marinated and grilled to perfection with aromatic spices, shish tawook, which are tender chicken pieces with a garlic hint, and kofta kebabs, minced meat skewers mixed with fresh herbs. The platter is completed with beef kebabs, offering a juicy interior with a beautifully charred exterior. This combination of meats delivers a rich, flavorful experience perfect for any grill lover.
